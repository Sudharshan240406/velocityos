# Demo Video Script

## Length

60-90 seconds

## 1. Landing Page

- Screen: open the homepage and scroll through the hero
- Narration: "This is VelocityOS, a startup-grade productivity platform designed to make deep work feel like stepping into a futuristic supercar cockpit."
- Transition: quick zoom into the primary CTA

## 2. Velocity Dashboard

- Screen: open `/workspace`
- Narration: "Inside the workspace, the product shifts from marketing to a premium driving-inspired dashboard."
- Transition: cut on dashboard reveal

## 3. Focus Session

- Screen: show the start button and timer boot-up
- Narration: "A focus session starts like an ignition sequence, with telemetry gauges for speed, RPM, turbo, and nitro."
- Transition: gauge sweep animation

## 4. Velocity Mode

- Screen: highlight the speedometer and session controls
- Narration: "Instead of a plain timer ring, Velocity Mode turns productivity into a cinematic, high-feedback control panel."
- Transition: pan across gauges

## 5. Garage Unlock

- Screen: switch to Garage panel
- Narration: "Each completed session earns XP that unlocks vehicles, turning consistency into visible progression."
- Transition: snap zoom to unlock cards

## 6. Achievement Celebration

- Screen: show the session completion overlay
- Narration: "When a session completes, VelocityOS celebrates it like a premium racing game reward sequence."
- Transition: overlay burst

## 7. AI Driver Coach

- Screen: switch to AI Coach panel
- Narration: "The AI Driver Coach analyzes patterns in your focus behavior and recommends what to do next."
- Transition: slide between insight cards

## 8. Analytics Dashboard

- Screen: open analytics and charts
- Narration: "Analytics show weekly performance trends, recent sessions, and productivity telemetry."
- Transition: chart crossfade

## 9. Mobile Experience

- Screen: responsive mobile viewport with bottom nav
- Narration: "The experience is mobile-friendly, installable as a PWA, and ready for on-the-go focus sessions."
- Transition: device frame cut

## 10. Closing Screen

- Screen: return to landing page or logo lockup
- Narration: "VelocityOS is what happens when deep work, gamification, and product design are treated like a premium launch product."

## Background Music Suggestions

- cinematic synthwave
- restrained electronic pulse
- premium automotive commercial ambience
