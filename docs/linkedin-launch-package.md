# LinkedIn Launch Package

## Short Launch Post

Built **VelocityOS**: a premium, automotive-inspired deep work platform that turns focus sessions into a supercar-style dashboard experience with garage unlocks, AI coaching, PWA support, and real production auth/sync foundations.

## Long Storytelling Post

I wanted to explore what would happen if a productivity app felt less like a utility and more like a product you would actually be excited to open every day.

That idea became **VelocityOS**: a deep-work platform inspired by high-end automotive dashboards and modern SaaS craftsmanship.

It includes:

- a Velocity Mode focus dashboard
- a garage progression system with unlockable vehicles
- an AI Driver Coach
- analytics and telemetry
- offline-first persistence
- demo mode, auth, and cloud-ready Supabase sync

One of the most satisfying parts was the performance journey. The original homepage shipped at **589 kB** first-load JS. After route splitting and targeted lazy loading, it now loads at **106 kB**, while the workspace sits around **127 kB**.

Built with **Next.js 15, TypeScript, Zustand, IndexedDB, Three.js-inspired motion direction, PWA support, Supabase, and Vercel Analytics**.

## Feature Highlights
